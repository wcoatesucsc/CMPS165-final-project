Title: Where Industries Vulnerable to the Trade War are Concentrated

Purpose:

The purpose of this project is to visually demonstrate how large of an impact a trade war between China and the United States may have on each nation. The visualization is intended for any groups affected by the tariffs and those concerned with global trade relations. The visualization can also aid economists in getting a better graphic representation of a potential trade war.

Existing Visualizations

  • Brookings Institute Visualization of Jobs Affected and Where(April 9, 2018)
    • This source only analyzes employment, and how that would be affected by the retaliatory Chinese tariffs. It also combines all the data. I think it would also be interesting to look at individual products or categories that are targeted by these tariffs in addition to examining the tariffs as a whole.
  • Governing Magazine Visualization of States affected by proposed tariffs (April 5, 2018)
    • This analyzes exports to China, which states have the largest share of exports to China of various goods.
  • S&P Global Analysis of Companies And Sectors Most Impacted By U.S.-Chinese Tariffs
    • This uses mostly bar graphs of changes in the stock market immediately following the announcement of the tariffs to make predictions about which industries will feel the greatest effects from the tariffs.
