How the Trade War May Affect America's Economy

William Coates: wcoates@ucsc.edu
GitHub Link

Objective

As you may know, the US and China have been going back and forth lately on proposing tariffs. The US announced a steep tariff on Chinese steel and aluminum a few months ago, following up with a much wider range of tariffs, and the Chinese government quickly responded with tariffs on many US agricultural goods, which could have a huge impact on American farming. In the last few days there has actually been some talk of lowering or rolling back these tariffs, but many are still in effect and the tensions are far from over. I'd like to visualize which goods/industries are being targeted, how many people they would affect, and how would they be affected.
