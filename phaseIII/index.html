Concentration of Industries Affected by a Trade War

The purpose of this project is to visually demonstrate how large of an impact a trade war between China and the United States may have. The visualization is intended for any groups affected by the tariffs and those concerned with global trade relations. The visualization can also aid economists in getting a better graphic representation of a potential trade war.
